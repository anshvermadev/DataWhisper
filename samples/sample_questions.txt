# Sample Questions for Testing DataWhisper

You can use these questions to test the LLM data analysis and charting capabilities for each of the sample CSV files provided in this directory.

---

## 📊 sales.csv
This dataset contains mock sales data, including orders, quantities, prices, statuses, product lines, and customer details.

**Test Questions:**
1. "What is the total revenue (sales) for each product line? Show me a bar chart."
2. "Which territory (EMEA, NA, Japan, etc.) had the highest total sales overall?"
3. "What is the average MSRP for Classic Cars versus Vintage Cars?"
4. "Show me the top 3 customers by total amount spent."

---

## 🎓 college_majors_salaries.csv
This dataset contains data on college majors, graduation rates, employment rates, and median salaries.

**Test Questions:**
1. "Which Major Category has the highest median salary? Plot this as a bar chart."
2. "What are the top 5 specific majors with the lowest unemployment rate?"
3. "Is there a correlation between the total number of graduates in a major and their median salary?"
4. "Show me a bar chart of the median salary for the top 10 most popular majors."

---

## ✈️ airline_safety.csv
This dataset tracks incidents, fatal accidents, and fatalities for various airlines across two time periods (85-99 and 00-14).

**Test Questions:**
1. "Which airline had the most total incidents between 1985 and 1999? Show a bar chart of the top 5."
2. "What is the total number of fatalities across all airlines from 2000 to 2014?"
3. "Did any airlines have zero fatal accidents in both time periods?"
4. "Show me a bar chart comparing the total fatal accidents in 85-99 vs 00-14 for Delta and Southwest."

---

## 🦠 global_covid_latest.csv
This dataset contains global COVID-19 statistics by country, including total cases, deaths, and recoveries.

**Test Questions:**
1. "Which 5 countries have the highest number of total cases? Please show a bar chart."
2. "What is the global average of total deaths per country?"
3. "Show me a bar chart comparing total cases between the USA, India, Brazil, and the UK."
4. "Which continent has the highest aggregate number of active cases?"
